## 1. Business Overview

Iffer's Pictures is a professional photography business based in Cliffside Park, NJ, specializing in event photography for milestone celebrations. The photographer, Jennifer Matone, covers engagements, baby showers, bridal showers, parties, family portraits, maternity, headshots, and baptisms/christenings.

**Target market:** Individuals and families in Bergen County's eastern corridor planning milestone events — engaged couples, expecting parents, party hosts, and professionals needing headshots.

**Brand voice:** Warm, personal, approachable. Content must reflect Jennifer's genuine connection with her clients. Avoid corporate or generic stock-photo tones.

## 2. SEO Goals & KPIs

## 3. Target Audience

### Primary Personas

**Engaged Couples (25-35)**
- Planning engagement shoots and wedding-related events
- Searching: "engagement photographer [town] NJ", "bridal shower photographer"
- Decision factors: portfolio quality, personality, availability, pricing

**New/Expecting Parents (28-40)**
- Planning baby showers, maternity shoots, baptisms, first birthdays
- Searching: "baby shower photographer near me", "maternity photographer bergen county"
- Decision factors: warmth, experience with kids, reviews

**Event Hosts (30-55)**
- Throwing parties, celebrations, corporate events
- Searching: "party photographer [town] NJ", "event photographer bergen county"
- Decision factors: professionalism, pricing, event experience

**Professionals (25-55)**
- Need headshots for LinkedIn, corporate use, personal branding
- Searching: "headshot photographer [town] NJ", "professional headshots near me"
- Decision factors: quality, turnaround time, pricing

### Cultural Considerations
Bergen County's eastern corridor has significant Korean (Palisades Park, Fort Lee), South Asian (various), and Hispanic (North Bergen, Fairview) populations. Content should acknowledge and welcome cultural event traditions (doljanchi, mendhi, quinceañera photography).

## 4. Geographic Strategy

### Hyper-Local Focus: Bergen County, NJ

**Approach:** City-specific location pages targeting "[service] photographer [town] NJ" long-tail keywords. Each location page includes local venue mentions, landmarks, and area-specific content.

### Primary Towns (Phase 1 — highest priority)

| Town | Why Priority | Key Venues/Landmarks |

| Fairview | Adjacent to home base | Low competition, easy win |

## 6. Timeline & Milestones

SEO is an ongoing, continuous effort. Phases represent focus areas, not hard deadlines.

### Phase 1: Launch Foundation (Pre-launch → Month 1)
- Fix sitemap/route mismatches (critical — 404s from sitemap)
- Schema markup consistency and completeness
- Metadata optimization for all existing pages
- Google Business Profile setup and verification
- Google Search Console setup
- Implement 6 primary location pages

### Phase 2: Content & Expansion (Months 1-3)
- Blog infrastructure and first 3 posts
- 8 secondary location pages
- Service-specific OG images
- Internal linking strategy
- First round of local citations

### Phase 3: Authority Building (Months 3-6)
- Consistent blog content (1-2 posts/month)
- Venue guide blog posts for each target town
- Review/testimonial generation strategy
- Monitor and adjust keyword targeting based on Search Console data

### Phase 4: Optimization & Growth (Months 6+)
- Content refresh based on performance data
- Expand to additional long-tail keywords
- A/B test meta descriptions for CTR improvement
- Build backlinks through local partnerships and vendor directories
